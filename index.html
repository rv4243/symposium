Prompt Craft

Prompt Craft

Description: This is an exciting AI-powered image recreation event organized by the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science as part of the Technical Event Series. The goal of this event is to challenge students to generate and refine AI-generated images with precision and creativity. Each participant will receive a QR code that, when scanned, reveals a hidden image. Using AI tools such as Copilot, Leonardo, Gemini, Image.art, Craiyon, participants must recreate the image as accurately as possible.

To add an element of challenge and fun, participants will compete in three timed rounds, requiring quick thinking, prompt engineering skills, and AI tool proficiency. The timed rounds, pushing participants to refine their AI-generated images efficiently. Scores will be updated after each round, and the highest scorer at the end of all rounds will be declared the winner. The competition encourages originality, innovation, and a deeper understanding of AI-powered image generation within the field of artificial intelligence and data science.


Rules:
  • Participants scan a QR code to receive an image to recreate using AI tools.
  • Only AI image-generation tools can be used, and prompts must be submitted with the final image.
  • Judges evaluate accuracy, creativity, and quality, with the highest cumulative score determining the winner.

Mind Vault

Get ready for a thrilling test of memory and quick thinking in the Mind Vault!

Mind Vault

Mind Vault Get ready for a thrilling test of memory and quick thinking in the Mind Vault! In this event, participants will be asked a series of questions, but there’s a twist – before answering the current question, they must first recall and answer the previous question correctly. This pattern continues until the final question, where they must answer both the previous and final questions. The challenge lies in remembering past answers while keeping up with new questions, making it a true test of memory, focus, and quick recall. Are you ready to echo your way to victory? Let me know if you want any refinements!

Rules:
  • Participants cannot use notebooks, mobile phones, or any reference materials during the event.
  • Each team must respond within 10 seconds for every question.
  • If a team gives three wrong answers, they will be eliminated from the competition.

Chain Logic

Description:The Connections Game is an exciting and engaging activity that challenges participants to identify the hidden link between a set of images. This game encourages teamwork, creativity, and logical thinking as teams analyze photos to find a common theme. The game is designed to be both fun and mentally stimulating, making it ideal for events, classrooms, and social gatherings.

Teams of two will compete by observing a series of images displayed in rounds. Each set of photos will have a hidden connection, and teams must quickly determine what ties them together. The team with the most correct answers at the end of the game wins.


Rules:
  • Each team must have exactly two members.
  • Each round will have a 3-minute time limit to find and submit the answer.
